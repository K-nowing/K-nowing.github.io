Injae Kim

I am currently at KAIST.

My research focuses on 3D computer vision, neural rendering, and 3D scene representation. Recently, I have been working on Neural Radiance Fields, 3D Gaussian Splatting, feed-forward 3D reconstruction, and robust visual understanding.

I have also worked on explainable medical image classification and point cloud learning. You can find my publications on Google Scholar.
