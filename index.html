Injae Kim

M.S & Ph.D Integrated Student in MLV Lab, advised by Prof. Hyunwoo J. Kim.

School of Computing at KAIST, Daejeon, Republic of Korea.

My research focuses on 3D computer vision, neural rendering, and 3D scene representation. I am interested in Neural Radiance Fields, 3D Gaussian Splatting, feed-forward 3D reconstruction, and robust visual understanding.

If you are interested in collaboration, opportunities, or just a quick chat, please feel free to reach out to me.
